About Stormly

In Copenhagen Street Food’s sailor bar you can quench the thirst with organic beer from Danish micro breweries and rum. Stormly has its name from a demolished house at Christiania, where the letters on the container come from. You can buy tobacco and CowCash – Copenhagen Street Food’s own currency here.
